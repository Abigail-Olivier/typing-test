# Typing Speed Test

A desktop application designed to measure and improve your typing accuracy and speed. It features a minimalist interface where users can choose distinct test modes, track their real-time accuracy, and view their results.

## Features

* **Flexible Test Modes:** Switch seamlessly between a timed countdown session or a page-completion target based on your preference.
* **Live Word Highlighting:** Highlights the active word and provides instant color feedback for correct or mistyped words the moment you hit the space bar.
* **Metrics Dashboard:** Delivers automated post-test calculations for your gross Words Per Minute (WPM), overall accuracy percentage, and total mistake counts.
